
While researching cybercrime, I was most surprised by how easily people fall victim to scams like phishing and UPI fraud, even when they seem aware of online risks. Many attacks succeed not because of technical complexity, but because they exploit human trust and urgency. For example, fake messages or calls pretending to be from banks or payment apps can trick users into sharing OTPs or clicking malicious links. I realized that even a small mistake can lead to serious financial loss or data theft. As a result, one habit I will personally change is being more cautious before clicking on any unknown links or sharing sensitive information online. I will always verify the source of messages and avoid responding to suspicious requests. This awareness will help me stay safer and also guide others in avoiding such cyber threats.
