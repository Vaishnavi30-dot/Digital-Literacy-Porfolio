Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

Do's

. Use a professional username and display name on academic and career-oriented platforms.

. Think before you post, especially about topics that might affect your reputation later.

. Check your privacy settings regularly and limit who can see your personal information.

. Share positive achievements, projects, and learning experiences to build your digital portfolio.

. Verify the authenticity of news or information before reposting or forwarding.

Don'ts

. Post abusive, discriminatory, or offensive comments about individuals or groups.

. Share your personal phone number, address, or financial details publicly.

. Upload photos or videos of others without their consent, especially in sensitive situations.

. Engage in arguments or harassment; report and block problematic accounts instead.
