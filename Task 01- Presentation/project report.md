I created this infographic using **Canva**, as it provides easy-to-use templates, icons, and design elements that help in making visually appealing content quickly. My design focuses on the concept of digital literacy and highlights its importance in today’s technology-driven world. It explains what digital literacy means, including the ability to find, evaluate, and use information online responsibly. The infographic also showcases useful digital tools such as Canva, Google Workspace, and Prezi, along with safe internet practices like using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and keeping software updated. Additionally, it emphasizes why digital literacy matters, such as staying safe from online threats, improving research skills, and preparing for future careers.

One thing I found interesting while making this infographic was how combining visuals with short text makes information easier to understand and more engaging. However, I found it slightly difficult to organize all the information neatly without making the design look cluttered. Balancing text, icons, and spacing required careful adjustment, but it helped me improve my design and presentation skills.
